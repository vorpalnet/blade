Is every endpoint taking calls?

The Proxy Balancer distributes calls across tiered pools of downstream endpoints, skipping any it believes are down — from its OPTIONS pings and from live 503s. Every engine node keeps its own independent view; this dashboard shows them all, side by side. When one node disagrees with the rest, that disagreement is the diagnostic: the path between that engine and that endpoint is the thing to look at.
